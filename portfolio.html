At the core, I use art as a way to convey emotions. Each work I do has an emotion or memory focused into it. As emotions are complex, so is my art. When I create abstract pieces, there is no focus, it is purely channeling emotions onto the canvas. More traditional pieces are very intentional, with every part of the artwork having layers of meanings, a story and reasons to be there.
